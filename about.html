Hello everyone！I'm zhangqingjun(XB)

Life and study take up most of my time, but my world extends far beyond that. Fitness is my way of relieving stress and shaping myself. Every workout fills me with a renewed sense of strength. Music, on the other hand, serves as the solace for my soul. It can transport me from the hustle and bustle to a tranquil shore.
